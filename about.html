I am a Geospatial Software Engineer with over four years of experience developing geospatial applications and data products across the public and private sectors. I recently started MAP-LAB, a suite of collaborative mapping tools, with a mission of bringing real-time collaboration to GIS.

I am currently a UI Software Engineer at Ursa Space Systems .

I earned a Graduate Certificate in Geospatial Programming & Web Map Development from Penn State.

With my background in Geography and Environmental Studies, I have worked in various roles providing GIS and other data analytics services. I have produced both professional and academic quality cartographic products featured in official reports and published papers.
