I am a geospatial professional bridging geography, planning, and programming. I have always loved maps, technology, and the uniquness of place. Since earning my Bachelors of Arts in Geography/Environmental Studies, I have worked in various roles providing GIS and cartographic services. I have produced both professional and academic quality cartographic products featured in official reports and published papers. I am currently a Planning Technician at the Chautauqua County Department of Planning & Development.
